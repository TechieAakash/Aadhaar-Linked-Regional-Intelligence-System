Frequently Asked Questions

What is the timeline for grievance redressal?
Normally, grievances are resolved within 15 working days. However, complex cases requiring field verification may take up to 30 days. You can track status using the SRN provided.
Can I file a complaint for someone else?
Yes, select "Others" in the "Grievance For" dropdown. You will need to provide their EID/UID details if available.
What documents are supported for upload?
We accept PDF, JPG, and PNG files. The file size should not exceed 2MB per document. Ensure the scanned copy is legible.
How do I track my previous complaints?
Use the "Check Status" link on the dashboard or call 1947 with your Service Request Number (SRN).
