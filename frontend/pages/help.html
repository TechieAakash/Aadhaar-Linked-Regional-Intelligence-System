Help & Accessibility Support

Welcome to the Help section of the Aadhaar Lifecycle & Regional Intelligence System (ALRIS). This page provides information on how to navigate the website, access content using assistive technologies, and view documents in various formats.

Sections of this Website

The ALRIS portal is organized into the following key modules:

  • Dashboard (Home): Provides a national overview of Aadhaar enrolment, update trends, and system health status.
  • Lifecycle Intelligence: Deep-dive analytics into demographic and biometric update patterns across states.
  • Forecasting: Predictive models for anticipated service demand over the next quarter.
  • Anomalies: automated detection of irregularities or potential fraud patterns in enrolment data.
  • Decisions/Recommendations: AI-driven actionable insights for regional administrators.

Accessibility Statement

We are committed to ensuring that the ALRIS portal is accessible to all users, regardless of device, technology, or ability. It has been built with an aim to provide maximum accessibility and usability to its visitors.

We aim to comply with WCAG 2.0 (Level AA) guidelines and the Guidelines for Indian Government Websites (GIGW).
